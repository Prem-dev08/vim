Our Manufacturing Process

Paper Covering Machine

specialized piece of equipment used to uniformly wrap electrical conductors—such as copper or aluminium round wires, rectangular strips, and foils—with high-quality insulating paper.The machine can accommodate a variety of insulating materials, such as:Kraft Paper,Crepe Paper,Nomex® Paper,Mica Paper

Fibreglass Covering Machine

designed to apply high-temperature resistant fiberglass yarn or tape over copper and aluminium conductors, including round wires, rectangular strips, and foils. This insulation process provides exceptional mechanical strength, thermal stability, and abrasion resistance, making it ideal for heavy-duty electrical applications.Adjustable wrapping speed and pitch for different conductor sizes.Uniform tension control for consistent insulation thickness.

Extrusion Machine

Extrusion Machine is a precision equipment used for applying a continuous and uniform layer of insulating material over copper or aluminium conductors. In this process, the conductor is fed through a heated extrusion head where a molten polymer (such as PVC, XLPE, or other insulating compounds) is forced around it using a screw-type extruder. The extrusion process ensures consistent thickness, smooth surface finish, and strong adhesion of insulation to the conductor.

Enamelling Machine

A vertical enamelling machine is a highly specialized industrial device that coats copper or aluminum wire with a thin, insulating enamel film. The machine's vertical orientation uses gravity to achieve a uniform and even coating, making it ideal for producing high-quality magnet wires used in electrical components like motors and transformers.Vertical orientation prevents the heavy wire from sagging under its own weight as it travels through the hot oven, ensuring an even coating.

Enemelling Section

The Enamelling Section at Vimlesh Industries is a dedicated production line where bare copper and aluminium conductors are transformed into high-quality magnet wires through a precision enamelling process. This section is equipped with modern enamelling machines, multi-zone curing ovens, and automated handling systems to ensure consistency, durability, and compliance with international insulation standards.
